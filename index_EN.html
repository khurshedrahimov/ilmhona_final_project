Portable motorized drill for obtaining oriented samples, PMB-2b

(manufactured in Russia)

The handheld motorized drill PMB-2b is designed for sampling geological, geophysical, geochemical, and other specimens, specifically for obtaining oriented paleomagnetic cores of standard diameter. To ensure maximum reliability in the design of the PMB-2b drill, a direct transmission of torque to the drill bit is used, while its autonomy is ensured by a two-stroke gasoline engine with 50 cubic centimeters and omnidirectional fuel supply for drilling at any angle. The device also includes a simple and reliable type of attachment for the drilling crown with an open tail, allowing for changing the drill bit without the use of additional tools. The PMB-2b drill is manufactured in Russia.

Portable tank with a manual pump

(For use with the PMB-2b handheld motorized drill)

This is a 10-liter tank with a manual pump designed for use with the PMB-2b handheld motorized drill. The kit includes a 3-meter hose with quick-release couplings. There are lugs on the tank for attaching a shoulder strap. The tank is suitable for carrying water and has a manual pump for easy dispensing.

Threadless drill bit with a diamond tip.

(manufactured in Russia)

The drill bit is made of high-strength, non-magnetic stainless steel. The diamond coating on the 12mm wide cutting edge, with radial cutouts, ensures high-quality and smooth drilling. The drill bit is designed for wet drilling. The diameter of the core obtained corresponds to the standard diameter of a paleomagnetic sample and typical orienting devices.
